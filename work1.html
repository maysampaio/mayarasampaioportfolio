Collage - Butterflies in stomach


This collage was created as a representation of the transformative power of falling in love. Love has the profound ability to alter our perception of the world and ourselves, opening doors to experiences and emotions that may have once seemed out of reach. The butterflies in the stomach, a universally recognized metaphor for the thrill of romance, symbolize the guidance love provides toward uncharted realms of sensation and feeling.

These butterflies invite us to dream of flying, exploring, and embracing the world with renewed curiosity and openness. They evoke the possibility of endless smiles, uncontainable joy, and a sense of happiness that resonates deeply within both the body and the heart. For those who allow themselves to be vulnerable and embrace love fully, these possibilities become more than mere dreams—they become lived realities.

Through this piece, I sought to capture the essence of love’s capacity to elevate and inspire, turning the ordinary into the extraordinary and reminding us of the beauty that lies in surrendering to the emotions that connect us.
