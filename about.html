ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Mayara Sampaio...but you can call me May.
I’m a Graphic Designer and UX/UI from Brazil. I have a background in Language and Literature (Portuguese) and Graphic Design, and I've worked with communication, social media, and marketing. Now, I'm into UX and UI.

Continuing to learn is a goal of mine, so I constantly seek to improve and acquire new knowledge, especially in subjects related to digital transformation, virtual experiences, and finding a balance between digital and analog to reach and include more people, aiming to make a positive impact.

I love a good conversation; chatting and getting to know others is a unique opportunity. These are the best moments for new ideas to emerge. Seeing others helps me see myself better. I'm also a big fan of art, so I'm involved in various activities like embroidery, painting, collage, and drawing, always exploring and learning.
